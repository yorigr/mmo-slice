# 🎮 MMORPG PROJECT MANIFESTO

> "A medieval MMORPG where skill matters, risk is real, and every death teaches a lesson."

---

## 🎯 OUR VISION

Build an MMORPG that captures the essence of **Shangri-La Frontier** - a world where:

- **Player skill is paramount** - No pay-to-win, no stat inflation, just raw gameplay
- **Risk = Reward** - Danger creates tension, and tension creates memories
- **Discovery > Spreadsheets** - Players should find broken builds, not read theorycrafting posts
- **Community matters** - Guilds rise, rivalries form, legends are born
- **Mistakes cost something** - Death should mean something, not "respawn 5 seconds later"

---

## 💎 THE 5 PILLARS

### 1. **Skill-Based** 
We believe progression should reward mastery, not time played.
- Equipment determines YOUR capabilities, not predetermines winners
- Same 5 skills = 100 ways to play
- A smart new player beats a careless veteran
- **No RNG on core mechanics** - Skill matters every single moment

### 2. **Hardcore Risk**
We believe consequences create meaning.
- Lose EVERYTHING on death in red zones
- Durability degrades on use & failure
- No safe spaces in PvP zones
- Death is a setback, not a "whoops" moment

### 3. **Transparent Balance**
We believe players should understand the math.
- All damage calculations documented
- Balance changes explained, not hidden
- Console can override balance for testing
- Community can verify "is this broken?" with data

### 4. **Anti-Exploit**
We believe fair play is sacred.
- Server validates 100% of player actions
- Exploits cost accounts permanently
- Glitches that enable unfair play are nuked fast
- "Skill-based" doesn't mean "speedhack-based"

### 5. **Medieval Low-Tech**
We believe in aesthetic consistency.
- No helicopters, no lasers, no sci-fi bullshit
- Swords, spells, and strategic positioning
- Castles matter, tech doesn't
- Cel-shaded anime aesthetic unifies the world

---

## ❌ WHAT WE'RE NOT

### ❌ NOT Pay-to-Win
No cosmetics that give advantage. No battle passes. No "convenience" items that matter.
**Our way:** Cosmetics are optional, never required.

### ❌ NOT Casual
Not everyone will get the top gear. Not everyone should beat the hardest bosses.
**Our way:** Difficulty is real, skill is rewarded.

### ❌ NOT Permadeath (Full Loot)
You die, you lose your items. You DON'T lose your character permanently.
**Our way:** Harsh punishment, but you can climb back up.

### ❌ NOT Auto-Pilot
No AFK farming, no macro-ing, no idle progression.
**Our way:** Every kill requires active engagement.

### ❌ NOT Spreadsheet-Based
You shouldn't need a calculator to understand damage.
**Our way:** Intuitive mechanics, transparent numbers.

---

## ✅ WHAT WE ARE

### ✅ Hardcore But Fair
- Risk is real, but not unfair
- Noobs have some protection (level-scaling)
- Veterans earn their dominance through time + skill
- Everyone can compete, not everyone will win

### ✅ Skill-Rewarding

### ✅ Community-Driven

### ✅ Transparently Developed

## 🎬 PHILOSOPHY: THE "AKATSUKI PRINCIPLE"

In Shangri-La Frontier, the main character **doesn't follow the meta**. He:
- Experiments with weird skill combos
- Discovers unorthodox strategies
- Uses game mechanics in unexpected ways
- Breaks the "optimal" path and finds his own

**We want OUR game to enable this.**

### Examples of the "Akatsuki Principle" in Action:

❌ **Wrong:** Game is "solved" (one optimal build everyone uses)  
✅ **Right:** 5 equally viable playstyles + 20 viable alternatives

❌ **Wrong:** Speedrunners are banned (they "break" the game)  
✅ **Right:** Speedrunners find cool routes, community copies them

❌ **Wrong:** Patch notes say "fixed exploit where players could do X"  
✅ **Right:** If X is skillful, leave it; if X is a bug, fix it

❌ **Wrong:** Balance changes because streamer said class is OP  
✅ **Right:** Balance changes because DATA says it's 15% stronger

❌ **Wrong:** One super-rare item breaks the metagame  
✅ **Right:** Rare items are good but not mandatory

## 🚫 RED LINES (WE WILL NOT CROSS)

### 🚫 Exploits for Power
If it breaks game logic → BAN the player, fix the exploit immediately.  
Examples: Speedhacking, infinite mana, teleporting through walls, damage clipping.

### 🚫 P2W Mechanics
If it gives unfair advantage for money → REMOVED immediately.  
Examples: Pay-for-power items, cosmetics affecting gameplay, event exclusivity.

### 🚫 Meaningless Progression
If leveling doesn't feel good → REDESIGNED.  
Examples: Grindy quests, boring skill trees, stat inflation.

### 🚫 Dead Guilds
If guilds feel pointless → EXPANDED.  
Examples: No guild wars, no territories, no rewards.

### 🚫 Broken Economy
If inflation is out of control → RESET or SINK introduced.  
Examples: Rampant RMT, gold farmers, item duping.
